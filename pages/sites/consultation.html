Choosing an energy/resource dashboard can raise many questions and is a significant undertaking. OED wants to help and understands that each site is unique. We also want you to make an informed decision that will meet your needs. We are here for you and the community and not to make money or sell our dashboard. With this in mind, OED offers a consultation to hear your thoughts and questions and hopefully provide appropriate information. Here are some details:

  • If you are a nonprofit organization then the consultation is free. If not, we can discuss an appropriate fee. We try to keep the cost low and any money collected is used to fund the development of the project (and not pay money to the person you consult with).
  • We strive to provide timely consultations with every organization that asks. As long as the total number of requests does not exceed our capacity as a volunteer driven project, OED will continue to do this. In any case, you can expect a timely response from the project.
  • The consultation will normally be with a senior member of the project who can discuss (almost) any aspect of the OED project and have experience with site needs.
  • The initial consultation is around 30 minutes. We can do additional ones if appropriate.
  • Contacting the OED project is not considered a consultation. Even after the initial consultation, OED remains available to help you with everything from dashboard setup, issues you encounter or other inquiries.

Scheduling a consultation

Please use the "Contact Us" link on our Contact page where you choose the "Consultation" topic/issue on the form. We will reach out to schedule a meeting.
